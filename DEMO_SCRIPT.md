# 🚀 Senpai: Executive Demo Script (Comprehensive Edition)
**Audience:** Top Otsuka Management
**Duration:** ~20–25 minutes
**Goal:** Prove that Senpai is a deeply integrated, deterministic enterprise sales co-pilot showcasing advanced agentic workflows, multi-modal ingestion, live browser crawling, and Graph RAG.

---

## ⏱️ 00:00 – Introduction: The Vision of Senpai
**Speaker Notes:**
> "Good morning. Today, we aren't going to show you a chatbot. Senpai is an active, intelligent partner built on three pillars: Execution, Experience, and Motivation. It understands our sales methodology, our CRM structure, and our past wins and losses. Let’s start on the front lines."

---

## ⏱️ 02:00 – ACT 1: The Junior Rep (Proactive Action & Live Intelligence)
**Screen:** Login as **Junior** → Go to `Home` (Command Center).

### Scene 1.1: The Morning Briefing (Proactive AI)
*   **Speaker:** *"When a rep logs in, they don't have to figure out what to do. They simply ask Senpai to brief them."*
*   **Prompt (Chat):** `Give me my morning briefing.`
*   **Visual:** The AI uses the `morning_briefing` tool. It ranks open deals by urgency and value, highlighting a deal going cold (e.g., no activity in 14 days) and suggests the next best action.

### Scene 1.2: Account Intelligence (`/account`)
*   **Speaker:** *"The rep sees they need to act on the Murata Printing account. To get up to speed, they invoke a specialized account-level workflow."*
*   **Prompt (Chat):** `/account Murata Printing`
*   **Visual:** The UI triggers a dedicated Account Intelligence stream. A senior "Account Manager" agent reads the entire relationship trajectory, expansion opportunities, and deterministic health scores.

### Scene 1.3: Live Website Crawling (`/intel`) — 🌟 THE BROWSER-SIM WOW
*   **Speaker:** *"Before calling the client, the rep wants to know what they are doing right now. They don't just do a Google search; they unleash Senpai to visually crawl their website."*
*   **Prompt (Chat):** `/intel Murata Printing's latest product launches` (or enter their URL).
*   **Visual:** The UI transitions into the **Live Browser Replay**. The executives literally watch the AI autonomously open a headless browser, navigate the customer's site, scroll through the pages in real-time, extract data, and synthesize a grounded intelligence brief!
*   **Speaker:** *"Senpai is actually browsing the internet for us, reading the screen, and building a custom intelligence packet live."*

### Scene 1.4: Walk-Out Voice Ingestion (Multimodal)
*   **Screen:** Switch to `Ingestion` page (`/junior/ingestion`).
*   **Speaker:** *"The rep goes to the meeting. Walking out, instead of typing a report, they upload a 10-second voice memo."*
*   **Action:** Upload an audio file (or text fallback).
*   **Visual:** The system extracts the text, maps products, structuring it into the exact SPR CRM schema. 
*   **Action:** Click **Save**. *"This instantly syncs to the CRM and the retrieval index."*

### Scene 1.5: Cross-Referencing Local Files (`/review` & `workspace`)
*   **Screen:** Return to the Command Center.
*   **Speaker:** *"The rep has a rough draft of a strategy document on their local computer. Let's see if it aligns with our CRM data."*
*   **Prompt (Chat):** `/review Check my local strategy document against the recent Murata Printing activities.`
*   **Visual:** The AI uses the `workspace` tool to read the local file, then calls `query_spr` and `search_notes` to cross-reference the local document with live CRM truth.

### Scene 1.6: One-Click Deliverables (`generate_proposal`)
*   **Prompt (Chat):** `Generate a proposal for the Murata Printing deal.`
*   **Visual:** The AI uses `generate_proposal`. Download chips for **PPTX** and **PDF** appear.
*   **Action:** Open the PPTX. *"A 9-slide, formatted Otsuka PowerPoint with exact financials, grounded in the CRM. A 2-hour task done in 15 seconds."*

---

## ⏱️ 12:00 – ACT 2: The Manager (Agentic War Rooms & Coaching)
**Screen:** Switch tabs. Login as **Manager** → Go to `Home` (`/manager`).

### Scene 2.1: The Single-Deal "War Room" (`/crew`)
*   **Speaker:** *"Managers oversee dozens of deals. When a deal looks risky on the dashboard, they can deploy a specialized crew of AI agents to analyze just that deal."*
*   **Screen:** Open Copilot Chat.
*   **Prompt (Chat):** `/crew Analyze the Fujimoto deal.`
*   **Visual:** The screen shows multiple agents spinning up: A **Researcher** and a **Coach** run in parallel, followed by a **Strategist** merging their findings. 

### Scene 2.2: The Team Forecast Fan-out (`/team`)
*   **Speaker:** *"What if the manager wants a forecast for the whole team?"*
*   **Prompt (Chat):** `/team`
*   **Visual:** Massive parallel execution. An Analyst agent spins up for *every single rep* on the team simultaneously, synthesizing a massive pipeline report. *"Infinite scaled management."*

### Scene 2.3: Faceted Search & Coaching Profiles
*   **Prompt (Chat):** `Find deals lost to competitors in the manufacturing sector over 10M yen.`
*   **Visual:** The AI uses `find_deals` (Faceted Search) to query structured tables directly.
*   **Screen:** Go to `Coaching` tab. Show Rep Profiles tracking longitudinal weaknesses matched with exact coaching "Principles" added by human managers.

---

## ⏱️ 18:00 – ACT 3: The Executive Admin (Graph RAG & System Pulse)
**Screen:** Switch tabs. Login to the **Admin Portal** (`/admin`).

### Scene 3.1: Deep Market Research (`/research`)
*   **Speaker:** *"Executives and strategists need market awareness. Senpai has a dedicated deep-research pipeline."*
*   **Prompt (Chat / Search):** `/research Give me a deep dive on Microsoft's latest retail sector acquisitions.`
*   **Visual:** The tool bypasses internal CRM and leverages the dedicated deep web-crawling pipeline to synthesize external intelligence.

### Scene 3.2: The "Mic Drop" — Live Graph-RAG vs Traditional AI
*   **Screen:** Navigate to `Visualization` -> `Live Graph-RAG` vs `Traditional` (`/admin/visualization/versus`).
*   **Speaker:** *"Why build this custom instead of buying a generic ChatGPT wrapper? Here is why."*
*   **Prompt (UI Input):** `Why are we losing retail deals?`
*   **Visual:** The screen splits.
    *   **Right Side (Traditional AI):** Returns a generic, shallow answer.
    *   **Left Side (Otsuka Graph RAG):** The graph visually animates! It traverses 44 custom-built mathematical communities, traces to specific reps, and pulls exact win-rates.
*   **Speaker:** *"Our Graph RAG traverses proprietary mathematical communities of Otsuka’s historical deals. It proves its answer with exact win-rates. It knows the shape of our business."*

## 💡 Presenter Cheat Sheet: Slash Commands
*   `/intel [URL or topic]` -> 🌟 **Live Browser-Sim!** Autonomously scrolls websites and extracts a grounded brief.
*   `/account [name]` -> Triggers a grounded account-rollup and senior manager commentary.
*   `/crew [deal]` -> Single-deal deep dive using parallel specialized agents (Researcher/Coach/Strategist).
*   `/team` -> Manager fan-out. Deploys one AI analyst per rep simultaneously.
*   `/research [topic]` -> Dedicated web-crawl and external synthesis pipeline.
*   `/review [prompt]` -> Triggers the workspace tool to review local/provided files against CRM context.
